Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in Rural Sociology and Demography. My research interest is focused on Migration, Environmental Demography, and their impacts on the population, environment, and dynamics between them. I also do spatial analyses of poverty and educational inequality. I have strong backgrounds in statistical programming languages such as Stata and Python.
